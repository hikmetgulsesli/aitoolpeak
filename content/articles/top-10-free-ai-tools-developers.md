# Top 10 Free AI Tools Every Developer Should Know

Not everyone has budget for premium AI coding assistants. Here are 10 free alternatives that deliver serious value.

## 1. GitHub Copilot Free Tier

60 completions per day for verified students and open source maintainers.

## 2. Continue.dev

Open-source AI coding assistant that works with any model.

## 3. Ollama

Run open-source LLMs locally for complete privacy.

## 4. Codeium

Free AI autocomplete with no usage limits.

## 5. Aider

Terminal-based pair programming with Git integration.

## 6. Tabby

Self-hosted AI coding assistant.

## 7. Supermaven

Free tier with 150k context window.

## 8. Sourcegraph Cody

AI assistant for understanding large codebases.

## 9. Amazon CodeWhisperer

Free for individual developers.

## 10. ChatGPT

The free tier works great for coding questions.

## Conclusion

You don't need to pay $20/month to benefit from AI coding assistance.
