# The Complete Guide to AI Tool APIs: Pricing Compared

Choosing the right AI API can significantly impact your project's budget. Here's a detailed pricing comparison.

## Pricing Models Explained

### Per-Token Pricing

Most AI APIs charge per input and output token:
- Input tokens: Your prompt
- Output tokens: The AI's response

### Per-Request Pricing

Some APIs charge a flat fee per API call regardless of size.

### Subscription Tiers

Monthly subscriptions with included usage limits.

## Hidden Costs to Consider

- **Embedding costs**: For RAG applications
- **Fine-tuning**: Custom model training
- **Rate limits**: May require higher tiers
- **Data transfer**: Egress fees

## Cost Optimization Tips

1. Use caching for repeated queries
2. Implement request batching
3. Choose the right model for the task
4. Monitor usage with alerts

## Conclusion

The cheapest option isn't always the best—consider quality, latency, and reliability.
