# How AI Is Transforming DevOps: Tools and Workflows

AI is reshaping every aspect of DevOps, from infrastructure management to incident response.

## Infrastructure as Code

AI can now:
- Generate Terraform configurations
- Optimize CloudFormation templates
- Suggest security improvements

## Monitoring and Observability

### Datadog AI

Intelligent anomaly detection and root cause analysis.

### New Relic AI

Natural language queries for your observability data.

## Incident Response

AI assistants can now:
- Diagnose issues from logs
- Suggest remediation steps
- Automate rollback procedures

## CI/CD Pipelines

GitHub Actions and GitLab CI now offer AI-powered workflow optimization.

## The Future

AI won't replace DevOps engineers, but engineers who use AI will replace those who don't.
