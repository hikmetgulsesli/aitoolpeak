# AI-Powered Monitoring: Tools to Watch Your Infrastructure

AI is revolutionizing infrastructure monitoring with predictive capabilities and intelligent alerting.

## The Problem with Traditional Monitoring

- Alert fatigue from too many notifications
- Reactive rather than proactive
- Difficult to correlate across systems

## AI-Powered Solutions

### Datadog Watchdog

AI-powered anomaly detection that learns your system's normal behavior.

### Dynatrace Davis

Causal AI that identifies root causes automatically.

### New Relic AI

Natural language queries and intelligent alerting.

### Grafana Machine Learning

Forecasting and anomaly detection for your metrics.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Anomaly Detection**: Identify unusual patterns automatically
2. **Root Cause Analysis**: Pinpoint the source of issues
3. **Predictive Alerting**: Warn before problems occur
4. **Intelligent Correlation**: Connect seemingly unrelated events

## Implementation Tips

- Start with baseline metrics
- Tune sensitivity to reduce false positives
- Integrate with your incident response workflow

## Conclusion

AI monitoring reduces MTTR and prevents outages before they happen.
